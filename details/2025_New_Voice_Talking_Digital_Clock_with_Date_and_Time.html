MASSII 2025 Voice-Talking Digital Clock with Date and Time

Experience the ultimate in timekeeping with our sleek and intuitive digital clock that combines clarity, convenience, and smart features. Designed to support seniors and children, this clock helps maintain a routine and streamline daily life with its advanced functions.

High-Resolution IPS Display

The vivid IPS screen features HD 1280x800 resolution, ensuring excellent visibility in all lighting conditions. Its clear and large text makes it easy to read for elderly users or those with memory challenges.

Voice Announcements for Clarity

With built-in voice talking functionality, the clock clearly announces the current time and date, reducing the need for constant visual checks. This is particularly useful for individuals requiring assistance with time awareness.

20 Customizable Alarms

Set reminders for birthdays, appointments, and important events with 20 alarms. Each alarm can be personalized to suit different schedules, making it a versatile companion for everyday life.

Auto DST and Night-Dimming Function

The clock automatically adjusts for daylight saving time, keeping your schedule accurate without any manual intervention. The 10-level dimmer also allows customization for night and morning settings, reducing glare and improving usability.

Designed for Dementia Care

Equipped with a built-in sleep trainer, the clock can be programmed to set specific night-dimming times, helping to promote better sleep patterns for those living with dementia or Alzheimer's.

Power-Adaptor and Backup Features

Enjoy uninterrupted timekeeping with the 1.5-meter power adaptor and outlet-powered design. It includes a cell battery backup to ensure continuous operation during power outages, eliminating the need for frequent battery changes.

User-Friendly Remote Control

Control your clock effortlessly with the included user-friendly remote. It's easy to use, making it a perfect addition for both children and the elderly who may need a simpler interface.
